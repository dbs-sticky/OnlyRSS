The problem with stacked bar charts

Linking a data table to its associated stacked bar chart

In the example below, each table column header is selected in-turn i.e. Total, In Service, and On Order. When a specific category in the table has been sorted, the bar chart is adjusted accordingly i.e. the selected category is moved to the chart's baseline, and sorted either descending or ascending, depending upon the sort direction chosen by the in the table.

In addition to the chosen category being moved to the chart's baseline, the category colour in the chart is set to the darkest colour, so it stands out when compared to the other categories.
