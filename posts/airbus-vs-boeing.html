Airbus vs. Boeing

My first attempt at creating a data visualisation using just HTML & CSS. This visual depicts the share of Boeing versus Airbus aircraft for the World's 10 largest airlines.

10 Dec 2022 · 2 mins · Mark Evans

Inspiration for this data visualisation came from the recent chart on VisualCapitalist.com which displays the share of men versus the share of women who smoke (for the world's 50 most populous countries).

Their chart was an image, I wanted to see if I could build a similar chart using HTML & CSS. I'm sure there are charting libraries that would let me build something similar, but I wanted full control of the design, and I also wanted to test my knowledge of CSS.

The result of my attempt is the chart below. In the chart, the vertical axis is used to show the airline's total fleet size, and the horizontal axis shows the percentage split between Boeing and Airbus.

If the chart were to be rotated 90°, and the elements shared a common baseline, then it would be a mosaic plot (or a Marimekko chart)
