When Bike? - Burghfield

Why does this page exist?

I created 'When Bike? - Burghfield' as a tool to help me decide the best time to go for a bike ride. It suggests the optimal 1, 2, 3, 4, and 5hr slots. I made it public on the off chance that it could be useful to other cyclists (or walkers) in the Burghfield area. About me

  • Data valid for: Friday 2nd May 2025

Best Bike Riding Time-slots:

  • 1hr: 1pm – 2pm. High temperature and solar radiation.
  • 2hr: 1pm – 3pm. Good temperature and solar radiation, increasing cloud cover later in the slot.
  • 3hr: 1pm – 4pm. Good temperature and solar radiation, and acceptable rain probability (3.2%) later in the slot.
  • 4hr: 12pm – 4pm. High temperature and solar radiation, despite increasing wind speed, cloud cover and rain probability towards the end of the slot.
  • 5hr: 11am – 4pm. Combines good temperature, high solar radiation (especially in the middle hours), acceptable wind speed, and low rain probability initially, balanced against increasing wind speed, cloud cover, and rain probability later in the slot.
