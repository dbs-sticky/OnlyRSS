When Bike? - Burghfield

Why does this page exist?

I created 'When Bike? - Burghfield' as a tool to help me decide the best time to go for a bike ride. It suggests the optimal 1, 2, 3, 4, and 5hr slots. I made it public on the off chance that it could be useful to other cyclists (or walkers) in the Burghfield area. About me

Best Bike Riding Time-slots:

  • 1hr: 4pm – 5pm. Highest bike temperature of 16.2°C, moderate solar radiation, and good visibility make this the best single hour.
  • 2hr: 4pm – 6pm. High bike temperatures (over 16°C), good visibility, and decreasing solar radiation are ideal for a shorter ride as sunset approaches.
  • 3hr: 3pm – 6pm. Good bike temperatures, moderate solar radiation, and good visibility contribute to a favorable 3-hour window, with slightly lower wind speeds later in the period. Rain probability is at 22.6% which may be a factor.
  • 4hr: 2pm – 6pm. High bike temperatures and high solar radiation, this is the first slot where rain probability may be a factor (22.6%).
  • 5hr: 1pm – 6pm. The bike temperatures and solar radiation are good and visibility is at it's highest. The only deterrent is the rain probability of 22.6%.
